### About

TEDxRUET is an independently organized TED event that is held annually at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET).
The official website for TEDxRUET serves as a platform for the team to showcase their upcoming and past events, sponsors, blog articles, speakers, team members, and more.

### Features

- Upcoming and past events
- Blog articles
- Speakers
- Team members
- Sponsors
- Contact form

### Tech Stack

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- [Sanity.io](https://www.sanity.io/)
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)

### Outline

The website is built with Next.js and Tailwind CSS. Sanity.io is used as the headless CMS. The website is hosted on Vercel.
